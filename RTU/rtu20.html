IIM Bangalore and Unmaad '20 presents Road To Unmaad


Unmaad 2020 invites all colleges, corporates and individuals to unravel mysteries and overcome challenges at the city's most exciting Treasure Hunt Competition!
"Guardians of Unmaad, assemble! Use the clues and hop to each pitstop! Can you do this all day and find your way"

Prizes worth INR 50K to be won

OVERVIEW: One bike. One day. One Goal. To save Unmaad'20. Armour yourself for the ultimate chase in the streets of Bangalore. Put on your superhero cape and unlock the clues as time runs out on you. Do you have what it takes to save the day?

Rules & Regulations:

1. THIS IS NOT A RACE! The event is all about having fun and exploring Bangalore. Remember to do that! And guess what, you get exciting prizes for it!
2. Each team MUST have two members and a two wheeler (bike/scooter) with a functioning odometer. Cars are not allowed.
3. Please ensure that you have relevant vehicle documentation.
4. The teams are requested to exercise caution on the road. Speeding is discouraged since time (We repeat!) is not a judging criteria. The organizing team will not take responsibility for any mishaps or vehicle breakdown during the event. The change of vehicle amidst the event is not acceptable and will lead to disqualification.
5. Each team will be given a set of 12 clues corresponding to 12 different locations (pit stops) across Bangalore. The team needs to decipher the clues and go to that pit stop and perform a fun task. The teams are free to choose the order of their pit stops.
6. The team will get points based on the task done and the highest scoring team wins.
7. The race starts at 10am in the morning and you must reach back to IIMB Campus by 5pm.
