Curtain Call

Register

Spin the thespian in you. It's Playtime baby!

Stage is a motion picture not restricted to 24 frames per second. Own the pulsating stage, weave the endless possibilities of theatre to captivate the audience and make them escape in the reality of your acts.

Prizes worth INR 80,000 to be won
Contact: Shadab: +91 7252803243
Shivam: +91 9933987367


1. A team should have a minimum of 3 actors. Apart from the actors, there can be max 5 persons for sound (including musicians) and lights.
2. The registration has to be made before Unmaad through the website www.unmaad.com; the last date for registration is 11:59 PM January 20th 2020.
3. The event is open only for colleges.
4. Each team needs to send a script and short performance video of actors. It is not required for the script in video to be same as the script shared.
5. Submission of scripts and video performance shall preferably be through soft copy via email to the ID - unmaad.events@gmail.com with the subject line 'Curtain Call_college name' by 11:59 PM 20th January 2020.
6. On the basis of the script and video, a total of six teams will be selected to perform at Unmaad. The shortlisted teams will be informed via email at least two weeks prior to the event.
7. Multiple entries per campus will be accepted. At most two teams per campus will be selected.

THEME
1. The script of the play can be original or adapted. Due credit should be given to the original playwright if the script is adapted.
2. Only English and/or Hindi languages are allowed.

DURATION
1. The teams will be allowed a maximum of 45 minutes to perform plus 5 minutes of stage set-up time.
2. Teams will be penalized for exceeding the given time limit of 45 minutes. No team will be allowed on stage beyond 50 minutes.

PROPS AND SOUND/LIGHTS
1. The teams should bring along people to manage sound and light.
2. Teams will be provided three mikes, six chairs and two tables – one small and one large. Other props should be arranged by teams on their own.
3. The teams are required to bring in their own music which will have to be uploaded on a google drive (details will be sent via e-mail to the selected teams) at least 3 hours before the event commences. Teams should also keep a back-up copy in a pendrive.
4. The organizers will be providing the equipment to play the music.
5. Use of fire (even diyas, candles or lighters) and liquid items are not allowed on the stage.

FINAL
1. Judge's decision will be final.
2. We reserve the right to change any of the rules.
