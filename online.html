Short Cuts

Register

Cut the long story short, and put the best shot forward!

So you think you are an auteur? Unmaad gives you the opportunity to showcase the inner Hitchcock inside you. Dazzle the audience with your vision that only a camera can bring out.

Prizes worth INR 21,000 to be won
Contact: Shadab: +91 7252803243
Shivam: +91 9933987367


1. A team should have maximum 10 core members. There is no limit on actors.
2. This is an online competition with two rounds. The short film submission can in any form (non-animation, stopmotion etc).
3. The registration for the online round is to be made before Unmaad through the website www.unmaad.com; the last date for registration is 11:59 PM January 23rd 2020.
4. The event is open only for colleges.
5. We reserve the right to change any of the rules.


Round 1:
1. The participants need to share their google drive / youtube link of their short film during their registration. The length of short film has to be 1 min - 2:30 min. Please ensure the link shared has open access.
2. Multiple entries per campus will be accepted.
3. There will be no theme for the submissions. However, the short film submitted should be thematically consistent.
4. The film can be in any language. If it is not in english or hindi, there should be subtitles. The entries will not be entertained if there will be no subtitles in such cases.
5. There will be deduction of 20% points if time exceeds up to one minutes. The team will be disqualified if the short film goes beyond 3:30 minutes in total.
6. The video format for final submission has to be in avi. There is no constraint on aspect ratio.
7. The teams will be shortlisted for round 2 based on the submission.
8. The judging criteria for round 1 is:
-Theme consistency
-Direction, Cinematography, Editing, Plotline
-X Factor (acting, use of other medium)

Round 2:
1. The short film of shortlisted participants will be shared on Unmaad facebook page. The Round 2 score will be calculated as:
-Scores from ROUND 1: 50%
-No. of likes in 120 hours from posting of video: 50%
