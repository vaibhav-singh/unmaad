1. The event is open to all participating colleges as well as corporates.
2. Each team will consist of 4 members – one participant each for the first two rounds, and 2 participants for the third round. Solo participation will not be permitted. Only one member from each team needs to register.
3. The event will have 3 rounds. Total event duration will be 2 hours.
4. Each round will commence only once the previous round has been completed. Once a team progresses to the next round, no participant from the team will be permitted to work on artwork from the previous round.
5. Participants will not be permitted to communicate with each other during a round, or between rounds. In round 2, members of the team will not be permitted to accompany the member working on the campus photography round.

9. Teams will be judged on design, theme integration, creativity and originality. Additionally, every minute saved from the overall event duration will win the team additional points.
10. Organizers hold the right to change/modify rules on the day of the event. Participants shall be informed in advance of any changes in rules and regulations specific to the event.
11. Decision of the judges will be considered final and binding in case of any conflict.
11. Registrations are accepted online. On the spot registrations on the day of the event can also be accepted depending on final number of participating teams. On the spot registrations will be on first come first serve basis.
