2. A team should have a minimum of 8 and a maximum of 25 members (including team members responsible for lights, sound, prop support). All team members must be from the same college/institution. Corporate teams are also allowed.

Get Set Solo

Register

20% Unconventional, 30% Undiluted, 50% Unabashed: 100% Unique

If you believe in sailing alone unanchored, get ready for a solo dance competition at Unmaad 2020. The stage is all yours to conquer, sail your way up to glory!

Prizes worth INR 20,000 to be won!
Contact: Smriti: +91 8208401943
Prunoti: +91 7349253337


1. This is a solo dance competition and all dance forms are welcome.
2. Registrations will be accepted online through the website and on the spot. It is advisable to apply online.
3. Participants may use props of their choice and the props must be arranged for by the participants themselves. The use of elements that may dirty the stage including fire, water, smoke, diyas, candles, matches or any other inflammable materials during the performance is strictly prohibited. Participants who do not leave the stage in a clean condition will be marked negatively.
4. Participants must submit their music on a USB drive (no cassettes etc. allowed). It is advisable to carry a back-up.
5. Participants must bring their own CD players/speakers for practice. No additional time/slot will be given for stage marking.
6. Participants can bring along individuals to support sound and prop requirements, if required.
7. Decision of the judges is final and binding in case of any conflict.
8. Organizers hold the right to change/modify rules on the day of the event.
9. Plagiarism will not be appreciated and will be marked negatively.
10. There will be three rounds.


Round 1: Grooves Don't Lie! (Elimination)
1. Contestants are free to put up any performance.
2. Contestants need to bring their own music for this round. The music could be a mashup of 1 or more songs.
3. Time limit: 1-2 minutes
4. Participants must submit their music on a USB drive (no cassettes etc. allowed). It is advisable that they carry a back-up.

Round 2: Fuse on the floor! (Semi-Final)
1. Contestants must showcase 2 contrasting genres of dance in their performance. Contestants will be required to tell the judges and audience about which genres they will be performing. Judges will accordingly score contestants on their versaitlity, technique and the fusion.
2. Contestants need to bring their own music for this round. The music could be a mashup of 1 or more songs.
3. Time Limit: 1-2 minutes.
4. Participants must submit their music on a USB drive (no cassettes etc. allowed). It is advisable that they carry a back-up.

Round 3: The Duel (Final)
1. This will be a face-off between any two randomly chosen participants, lasting for 3 minutes. The order in which participants will have to perform will be chosen randomly on-spot.
2. Participants in each randomly chosen pair will dance alternatively on the same track for about 45 seconds during the 3 minutes.
3. Vulgarity and obscenity will not be tolerated and can lead to disqualification.
